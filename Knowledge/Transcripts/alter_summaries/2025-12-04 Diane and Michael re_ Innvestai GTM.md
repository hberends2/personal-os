## Private Notes

Here's a summary of hotel transaction volumes in 2023 and 2024:

**United States:**

- **2023:** The LWHA survey reported approximately 340 single asset sale transactions over $10 million, totaling just over $12.8 billion.
- **2024:** The LWHA survey included 356 single transactions over $10 million, totaling just over $14.3 billion. JLL research, through Q3 2024, indicated a total transaction volume of $15.4 billion, a 13.1% decrease compared to 2023. However, single-asset volume reached $14 billion, marking an 8.1% decrease from 2023.

**Global:**

- **2023:** JLL's Hotels & Hospitality Group noted that global hotel performance soared, reaching full recovery, with RevPAR surpassing 2019 levels by 12%. In the Asia Pacific region, transaction activity remained steady in 2022 (fiscal year 3Q 2022 to 2Q 2023), with a 4% increase in transaction volume, reaching approximately $13.3 billion USD.
- **2024:** JLL anticipates that global hotel investment volume will accelerate, exceeding 2023 by 15% to 25%. In Europe, H1 2024 saw transaction activity increase to the highest levels since 2019, reaching €10.6 billion, a 123% increase year-on-year and already reaching the total volume for the whole of 2023. The UK recorded a total transaction volume of £6.3 billion, which is 31% ahead of the ten-year average.

Dealpath primarily focuses on managing deal pipelines and centralizing deal information, but it also offers features that support valuation analysis.

Here's a breakdown:

- **Deal Pipeline Management**: Dealpath is designed to streamline real estate investment and asset management processes by offering tools for managing deal pipelines, tracking performance metrics, and facilitating team collaboration.
- **Integration with Financial Models**: Dealpath can be configured to ingest model outputs, allowing deal teams to track updates and view projections for real-time decision-making. It also integrates with financial modeling tools to enhance investment analysis and forecasting.
- **Underwriting Model Comparison:** You can upload underwriting models and compare different financial scenarios to understand which deals are viable based on changing conditions.
- **Proprietary Comparables Database:** Dealpath helps you instantly surface comparables to benchmark deal financials, identify market or sector trends, and drive decision-making and strategy.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: The platform provides real-time analytics and reporting, so users can make informed investment decisions based on current market trends and asset performance.

While Dealpath doesn't directly support model creation or manipulation, it can ingest model outputs to help deal teams track updates, view projections, benchmark deals, and compare scenarios.

## Enhanced Notes

### InnVestAI Product Overview & Market Position

- Hotel valuation tool addressing Excel-based underwriting inefficiencies
	- Excel models have up to 80% error rates according to research papers
	- Manual process takes 7 days vs. hours with automation
	- Example: Wall Street firm had $1 billion miscalculation in deal analysis
- Competitive advantage through speed in deal evaluation
	- Analysts can review 5-6 deals per day vs. 3-4 per week currently
	- Early offers beat competitors still using manual Excel processes
	- Tool provides same valuations as Excel but dramatically faster turnaround

### Target Market & User Personas

- Seven distinct user personas identified for hospitality investment sector:
- Market differentiation from multifamily focus
	- Hotel investors rarely cross into other asset classes
	- Requires entirely new models, not simple tweaks to existing platforms
	- Competitors like Rediq and Archer would need complete rebuilds for hospitality

Sources: https://lwhospitalityadvisors.com, https://hotelinvestmenttoday.com, https://hotelnewsresource.com, https://jll.com, https://hvs.com, https://knightfrank.com

### Competitive Landscape Analysis

- Direct competitors primarily in multifamily space
	- Rediq (lowercase r): more manual, underwriting platform, withering market position
	- Archer: more automated than Rediq, middle-tier automation
	- Both exclude lenders from their target markets
- Adjacent tools not direct competitors
	- Dealpath: pipeline/asset management, not valuation tool
		- Users include real estate investment teams, commercial firms, portfolio managers
		- Focuses on deal pipeline management vs. model creation
		- Integration capabilities with financial models but doesn’t create valuations
		- Sources: https://selecthub.com, https://dealpath.com, https://softwaresuggest.com
- Hotel industry specificity creates natural barriers
	- Financial statements not standardized across properties
	- Different line item descriptions and column headers by owner/manager
	- No existing hospitality-specific automated valuation tools

### Product Evolution & Future Automation

- Current MVP focuses on financial data extraction and mapping
	- Eliminates copy-paste from Excel workflows
	- Users input assumptions while system handles data processing
- Advanced automation roadmap includes:
	- AI-powered assumption pre-population (interest rates, market data)
	- Agentic AI for comprehensive property research
	- Automated insurance rates, tax information, demographic data
	- Integration of multiple third-party data sources into single platform
- Additional functionality potential
	- Asset management capabilities for post-acquisition monitoring
	- Performance tracking against loan requirements and market benchmarks
	- Global scalability (same mechanics, different currency symbols)
- Strategic positioning as data consolidation platform
	- Current process requires multiple third-party sources (CoStar, insurance providers)
	- All client data and research results stored in InnVestAI database
	- Example: Costa Mesa hotel research completed in 2 minutes with comprehensive competitor and tax data
